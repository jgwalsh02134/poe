Poe Search: Containment Perimeter

Point Last Seen: 162 Menands Road / Orchard Grove • Missing Sunday, July 12, about 9 PM

Operational objective

Detect Poe crossing out of the search area, record her direction, and redirect search teams. This is soft containment: volunteers observe from safe public locations and never block roads, trespass, enter railroad property, or stop traffic.

Search hierarchy

  1. Core: PLS property, Orchard Grove, Barry Court, Little Lane and Patroon Place.
  2. Highest-probability corridor: Weare C. Little Memorial Park/Little’s Lake, its trails, shoreline and wooded drainage.
  3. North/northeast: Schuyler Hills, Turner Lane, Schuyler Road, Kromme Kill and Beth Emeth Cemetery.
  4. East: Albany Rural Cemetery, Moordenaers Kill, ravines, internal roads and ponds.
  5. South/east hazard sector: NY 378, Ganser-Smith Park, Broadway, rail corridor, Menands Bridge and Hudson River.
  6. West buffer: residential streets toward Loudon Road/US 9.

Containment-post procedure

  • Use pairs; remain on public property or with permission.
  • Watch quietly from a parked vehicle or fixed location.
  • If seen: record exact time, pin and direction; photograph/video; call 518-860-2077.
  • Do not create a crowd. One familiar person may call “Poe” and offer food while others stay back.
  • If Poe enters a highway, bridge or active railroad area, call 911.

Recenter rule

A credible sighting becomes the new temporary LKP. Place observers one travel junction ahead of her direction, move the inner search ring to that sighting, and preserve the original outer perimeter until two later sightings establish a new direction.
